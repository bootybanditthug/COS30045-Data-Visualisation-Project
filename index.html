Key Takeaways

What does 15 years of enforcement data tell us about whether cracking down on road offences actually works? Here are the three headline findings.

📊

More Testing, Lower Positivity

States that increased their breath-testing volumes saw a measurable decline in positive test rates over the long term.

📱

Mobile Fines Surged

Mobile phone fines grew faster than any other category after 2012, reflecting new legislation and camera-based enforcement technology.

🗺️

States Vary Widely

Enforcement intensity and outcomes differ significantly between states, suggesting policy differences play a major role beyond just volume.
